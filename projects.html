My Projects

Dial – Emergency Alert App

Created a silent emergency-alert app concept designed for individuals who cannot safely call 911. The project included market analysis, a functional feature set, B2C and B2G revenue models, and a pitch demonstrating how technology can improve real-time public safety.

Remington 870 Supply Chain Case Study

Completed a comprehensive supply chain analysis of the Remington 870 shotgun, examining supplier relationships, defect rates, inventory patterns, global sourcing opportunities, and risk mitigation strategies while proposing measurable improvements in quality and efficiency.

Bias in Design – UX Project

Designed a visual UX project explaining how reverse psychology and cognitive biases influence interface design. Used real examples and visual storytelling to show how design elements shape user behavior and how ethical UX choices promote clarity and trust.
